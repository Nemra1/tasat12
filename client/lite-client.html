Introduction

Lite chat client is extremely small in filesize (only about 60 kb) and still has the basic chat functions, including chat logo, user list, chat input area and chatbox to display chat content.

Benefits of the Lite Client

  • Fast loading speed because the decoration graphics are removed.
  • High load support.
  • 1000 people ax maximum in one single room.
  • Can be easily embedded into any webpage thanks to the small dimension, or dock at the right bottom of users’ browsers, or pop up a separate and resizable chat window.
